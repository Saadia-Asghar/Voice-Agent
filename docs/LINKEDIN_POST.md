# Go Viral Award post

The cheapest repair quote nearly won. It was also the most expensive.

In 24 hours at Hack-Nation’s Global AI Hackathon, I built **BenchDial** for the ElevenLabs “Negotiator” challenge: an AI voice buyer for laboratory-equipment repair.

BenchDial creates one confirmed repair scope, gives that identical ScopePrint to every provider, exposes hidden fees, negotiates only with evidence it can prove, and ranks the result using both cash cost and laboratory downtime.

The detail I care about most: a call is never labeled “recorded live” just because the browser session ended. BenchDial waits for the signed ElevenLabs webhook and a persisted transcript. No fake live states. No invented competing bids. No purchase authority.

Built solo with ElevenLabs Agents, React, TypeScript, Supabase, and Vercel.

Demo: https://hacknation-lemon.vercel.app

Repository: https://github.com/Saadia-Asghar/Voice-Agent
