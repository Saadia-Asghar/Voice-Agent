# BenchBid Demo and Submission Package

Official judging frame: **creativity, technical depth, presentation**. The video should make one memorable creative claim, visibly prove the signed evidence chain, and maintain a single clear story.

## One-line pitch

BenchBid is the evidence-backed AI voice buyer that turns one confirmed laboratory repair scope into comparable provider calls, truthful negotiation, and a human-reviewable award memo.

## Why it is not a wrapper

- ScopePrint makes every provider quote the same canonical specification.
- The Honesty Firewall validates competing-bid leverage outside the model.
- Structured endings prevent vague calls from becoming fake quotes.
- The Concession Ledger proves what changed, why it changed, and which transcript turn supports it.
- Downtime-adjusted ranking exposes the operational cost hidden by headline prices.

## Submission copy

### Problem

Laboratories lose money twice when equipment fails: first through downtime, then through incomparable phone quotes that omit necessary work. Teams repeatedly describe the same fault and still cannot prove why one offer is better.

### Solution

BenchBid creates one voice-and-document ScopePrint, calls three providers with that identical specification, itemizes every commercial and operational term, negotiates only with verified leverage, and produces an evidence-linked award memo.

### Technical implementation

React and TypeScript provide the four-stage decision interface. ElevenLabs Agents power voice intake and buyer/counterparty conversations. Supabase Edge Functions mint private WebRTC tokens and verify post-call HMAC webhooks. PostgreSQL stores canonical scopes, call provenance, transcripts, analysis, and an idempotent event ledger. Deterministic TypeScript ranks cash and downtime-adjusted outcomes.

### Responsible AI

The agent discloses that it is AI, never invents bids or authority, refuses binding purchase actions, records unknown values explicitly, and distinguishes live recordings from simulated fixtures throughout the interface.

## Completion gate

The software and submission narrative are ready. The entry becomes challenge-complete only after the three real consenting calls and one genuine leverage-caused concession are recorded and linked in the production database.
