# Hack-Nation official announcements: ScopeDial action plan

Source: Hack-Nation Discord announcement export supplied by Saadia Asghar on July 19, 2026.

## Rules and opportunities confirmed

- Build within one of the six official challenges. ScopeDial enters **Challenge 1: ElevenLabs — The Negotiator**.
- Solo participation is allowed; maximum team size is four.
- The top three projects in every challenge win.
- Judges score exactly three dimensions: **creativity, technical depth, and presentation**.
- The organizer’s execution advice is equally direct: solve a real problem, show measurable impact, and make it look good.
- The project video should demonstrate the product clearly, tell an engaging story, feel interactive, and use music or creative editing where helpful.
- Go Viral Award: publish on LinkedIn, tag Hack-Nation, and maximize reactions by **July 19, 2026 at 9:00 AM ET**. In July, that is 1:00 PM UTC / 6:00 PM Pakistan time.
- Discord timestamps are viewer-local. Use the Hack-Nation platform countdown as the authority for the submission hard close.

## Judge scorecard

| Criterion | ScopeDial proof | Show in the video |
|---|---|---|
| Creativity | ScopePrint, Honesty Firewall, Concession Ledger, and downtime-adjusted awards turn voice into a defensible buying decision | Open with the broken centrifuge and reveal why the cheapest quote loses |
| Technical depth | ElevenLabs WebRTC, short-lived server tokens, conversation-ID binding, signed HMAC webhooks, server-only evidence tables, structured outcomes, and deterministic ranking | End a real call, verify the signed transcript, then open its evidence receipt |
| Presentation | Four-stage visual story, one vertical, one scope hash, three provider behaviors, one measurable concession, one human decision | Keep one narrative and remove setup/debugging from the recording |

## Non-negotiable completion gates

- [ ] Record three genuine, consenting live sessions with distinct counterparty behavior.
- [ ] Confirm each lane changes to `RECORDED LIVE RUN` only after signed webhook verification.
- [ ] Capture at least one unscripted measurable change caused by truthful gathered leverage.
- [ ] Preserve the conversation IDs, full transcripts, permitted audio, itemized terms, and structured ending.
- [ ] Record the final product video from the deployed URL.
- [ ] Submit repository URL, deployed URL, video URL, concise description, and challenge selection.
- [ ] Publish the LinkedIn post before the Go Viral deadline if still open.

Fixtures are presentation backups only. They must remain visibly labeled and cannot satisfy the live-call gates.
